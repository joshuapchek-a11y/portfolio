STUDIO 5A MURAL

Studio 5a is a place for learning visual communication. But what does learning visual communication look and feel like? On one hand, there are formal graphic design principles and qualities that are drilled into us; none more iconic than the grid. On the other hand, visual play, material experimentation and ‘informal’ visuals are encouraged. The mural addresses the tension between these two aspects of studying and learning Visual Communication in Studio 5a.

mural mockup, spray paint typography with digital collage elements
